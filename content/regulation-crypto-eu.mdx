The EU has introduced updated regulatory frameworks that affect crypto service providers. Compliance teams must pay attention to AML/KYC requirements and reporting obligations.

### Action items for startups

- Review onboarding flows for KYC compliance.
- Ensure record-keeping meets retention requirements.
- Engage with legal counsel early to classify token models and services.

Proactive compliance can reduce friction with banks and payment processors while building trust with users.
